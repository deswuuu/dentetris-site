About Dentetris

Dental office construction and practice support, under one team

Dentetris is a dental clinic solutions company based in Oakville, Ontario. Since 2019, we have completed over 100 clinics across the GTA and Southern Ontario, combining 30 years of collective project management experience with deep knowledge of dental-specific construction requirements.

We handle the full scope of a dental office project under one contract — design, architectural drawings, permits, construction, equipment coordination, and practice setup. Beyond the build, Dentetris offers practice management consulting, staff training, and marketing support for clinics preparing to open or looking to improve how they operate.

We work with general dentists, orthodontists, oral surgeons, pediatric dentists, periodontists, endodontists, and multi-specialty groups at every stage of growth — from first-time startups to multi-location expansions.

Clinic Design & Construction

What every dental buildout includes

New Dental Office Buildouts

Space planning and test fits for retail, office, or medical units. Concept design, 2D and 3D layouts, and operatory planning. Full coordination of plumbing, electrical, HVAC, medical gas, and IT infrastructure for dental equipment.

Renovations & Expansions

Modernizing reception, treatment rooms, sterilization, and staff areas. Reconfiguring layouts to improve patient flow and productivity. Phased construction available to reduce downtime for practices that need to stay open during the work.

Interior Design & Finishes

Material and finish selection for durability, infection control, and the clinic's brand. Lighting that supports clinical tasks and a calmer patient environment. Custom millwork and dental cabinetry for operatories, sterilization rooms, and reception.

Permits, Compliance & Inspections

Full architectural drawing packages for building permits. Direct coordination with municipal building departments and inspectors. Accessibility, X-ray shielding, and Ontario healthcare building code requirements addressed from the design phase.
